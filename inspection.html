Thermal Mapping

Temperature Mapping is the process of testing performance of temperature controlled equipment and facility.

Southern Metrology and Calibration Services Inc. offer temperature mapping of;

    • Refrigerator
    • Freezer
    • Incubator
    • Oven
    • Liquid Bath
    • Ware House
    • Cold Storage/Chiller
    • Reefer Truck/Van
    • Temperature Enclosure

Note: All certification procedures shall be in compliance with the following standards

  1. NSF/ANSI 49 - Biosafety Cabinetry: Design, Construction, Performance, and Field Certification
  2. EN12469:2000 - Biotechnology. Performance criteria for microbiological safety cabinets
  3. AS1807:2021 Separative devices - Biological and cytotoxic drug safety cabinets, clean workstations and pharmaceutical isolators - Methods of test
  4. ASHRAE 110-2016 - Methods of Testing Performance of Laboratory Fume Hoods
  5. ISO 14644-1:2015 - Clean Rooms and associated controlled environments
