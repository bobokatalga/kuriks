About Us


"Southern Metrology and Calibration Services, Inc. is a reputable PAB accredited calibration laboratory to ISO/IEC 17025:2017, providing comprehensive metrology and calibration services."

Introduction

Southern Metrology and Calibration Services, Inc. is located at Artica Building, National Highway corner Almazora Street, Canlalay, Binan City, Laguna. Established in 2015, the company has quickly become a trusted name in the industry, offering comprehensive metrology and calibration services to customers across various sectors. As the calibration laboratory in the Philippines to be accredited by the PAB accredited calibration laboratory to ISO/IEC 17025:2017, Southern Metrology and Calibration Services, Inc. has a track record of delivering exceptional quality and accuracy. With a customer-centric approach, state-of-the-art facilities, and a highly ìntelligence staff, the company guarantees top-notch performance and professional quality services.

Services

Southern Metrology and Calibration Services, Inc. specializes in providing precise and reliable metrology and calibration services tailored to the specific needs of customers. With a wide scope of accreditation, the company offers calibration services in diverse fields, including thermometry, hygrometry, mass, balances, force, pressure, torque, analytical, environmental, volume, dimensional, electrical, frequency, density, and viscosity. The services ensure that measurement procedures meet the highest quality standards, providing customers with confidence and accuracy in their processes.

Facilities and Equipment

Southern Metrology and Calibration Services, Inc. is proud to possess the largest private laboratory facility in the country. With a spacious and well-equipped facility spanning a laboratory area of 1200 square meters across three floors, the company has the capacity to handle calibration projects of any size and complexity. The state-of-the-art laboratory is equipped with cutting-edge equipment, ensuring accurate and precise measurements across various calibration fields.

Personnel and Expertise

The team at Southern Metrology and Calibration Services, Inc. comprises highly skilled personnel who have undergone extensive training both domestically and abroad. They possess in-depth knowledge and expertise in their respective calibration fields. The personnel have received training from prestigious institutions, including the National Metrology Institutes, and are recognized as experts metrology of ISO/IEC 17025:2017. This ensures that the calibration services adhere to the highest industry standards. Additionally, the personnel undergo continuous training to stay updated with the latest advancements in metrology and calibration practices.

Accreditations and Compliance

Southern Metrology and Calibration Services, Inc. have gained recognition for their commitment to quality and compliance with regulatory bodies. As a calibration laboratory to receive accreditation from PAB accredited calibration laboratory to ISO/IEC 17025:2017, the company has set a benchmark for excellence in the industry. Compliance with regulatory requirements ensures that calibration services meet international standards and best practices. With more than 25 approved signatories, the company has a team of experts who oversee and certify the accuracy and reliability of calibration processes.

Additional Services

In addition to core calibration services, Southern Metrology and Calibration Services, Inc. offer additional services to support customers' needs. These include training on management systems and calibration to enhance understanding of industry standards and procedures. The company also serves as a proficiency testing provider, enabling assessment of the competence and accuracy of measurement processes. Furthermore, Southern Metrology and Calibration Services, Inc. provide inspection and certification services for advanced biosafety cabinets, fume hoods, microscopes, as well as thermal and relative humidity validation.

The dedicated team is ready to assist and provide expert guidance in metrology and calibration.
